Introduction

    WHO has declared Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) as epidemic affecting 184 countries as Pandemic. The first case of the 2020 coronavirus pandemic in India was reported on 30 January 2020 in Kerala State, originating from China.

    A on-wiki page was created in Wikidata under WikiProject India to co-ordinate the activities as the disease is spreading rapidly in different states of India.

    This dashboard is created to visualize data that are added to Wikidata related to coronavirus in India and it's States / Union Territories
